About Us

Welcome to OnlyBowling

Located in the heart of the Netherlands, OnlyBowling is the premier destination for bowling enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Our state-of-the-art facilities and commitment to customer satisfaction make us the top choice for both casual bowlers and competitive leagues.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a fun, safe, and inclusive environment where people can enjoy the sport of bowling. Whether you're looking to improve your game, host a memorable event, or simply have a great time with friends and family, OnlyBowling is the place to be.

Our Values

  • Customer Satisfaction: We strive to exceed our customers' expectations with exceptional service and facilities.
  • Community: We are committed to fostering a sense of community and providing a welcoming atmosphere for all.
  • Excellence: We aim for excellence in everything we do, from maintaining our lanes to organizing tournaments.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek to improve and innovate our services to provide the best experience possible.
